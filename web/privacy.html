Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2025

✓ Zero data collection. Devbench does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All processing happens locally on your Mac. No network calls are made by the app. No analytics, no telemetry, no tracking — of any kind.

1. Information We Collect

None. Devbench does not collect any personal information, usage data, diagnostic data, or any other form of telemetry. The app has no analytics SDK, no crash reporter, and no third-party tracking libraries.

2. How We Use Your Data

We don't. Since Devbench collects no data, there is nothing to use, process, or analyze. All data you interact with (clipboard contents, pasted text, file references) remains entirely on your machine and is processed in-memory only.

3. Network Calls

Devbench makes zero network calls. The app does not connect to any server, API, or cloud service. It does not phone home, check for updates automatically, or send usage statistics. The only time your Mac reaches out to the internet is if you click a link (such as a support URL or purchase page) — and that happens through your default web browser, not through Devbench itself.

4. Clipboard Access

Devbench accesses your system clipboard only when you interact with its tools. Clipboard contents are processed entirely in local memory and are never stored, logged, or transmitted. You remain in full control: nothing is copied or read without your explicit action.

5. Local Storage

Devbench may store minimal configuration preferences locally on your Mac (e.g., window position, theme preference, custom keyboard shortcuts). These settings are stored in standard macOS preference files and are never shared with us or any third party. No other data is persisted.

6. Third-Party Services

Devbench itself contains no third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. If you choose to purchase a license, payment processing is handled by Stripe or Gumroad, both of which operate under their own privacy policies. We do not receive or store your payment details.

7. Children's Privacy

Devbench is a developer utility tool and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from any user, regardless of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since Devbench collects no data, any changes will relate to clarifying or improving the transparency of this statement — never to introduce data collection retroactively.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or Devbench's data practices, please contact us at:

support@naxiai.com

Mac App Store Compliance

This privacy policy is provided in compliance with Apple's Mac App Store review guidelines. All apps submitted to the Mac App Store must have a readily accessible privacy policy. Devbench's policy is simple because the app is simple: no data collection, period.
